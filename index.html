Systematic Elimination

What We Know It Isn't

Every hypothesis we rule out narrows the search space. This is our current map — built from data, not intuition. It grows with every paper published.

Eliminated

Random or Gibberish Text

BPE morpheme structure does not collapse under character-level perturbation the way random text does. The structural signal is real.

Eliminated

Tagalog or Philippine Languages

Boundary Concentration mismatch (BC 0.20 vs Voynich 0.361). VMML is unstable across texts — cross-text Δ = 0.336 units, larger than the discriminant zone itself.

Eliminated

Any Tested Austronesian Language

Indonesian (4.838), Malay (5.293), Cebuano NLLB (5.609) — all fall below the alphabetic ceiling. No Austronesian language replicates the BC profile.

Eliminated

Schinner's Stochastic Model (2007)

Random EVA generation fails to replicate the full 3-metric structural profile simultaneously. The manuscript is more structured than the model predicts.

Eliminated

Timm's Self-Citation Mechanism (2020)

EBNF/MI trade-off is irreconcilable with observed structural patterns. The self-citation model produces a different structural signature.

Eliminated

63+ Natural Language Profiles

No tested corpus from any language family simultaneously occupies the Voynich discriminant zone on all three metrics (VMML, BC, CBMI). The zone remains empty.

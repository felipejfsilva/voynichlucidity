What We Know It Isn't

Each eliminated hypothesis narrows the search space. Below is our current map of ruled-out possibilities — built from empirical data, not speculation.

Eliminated

Random or Gibberish Text

BPE morpheme structure does not collapse under perturbation as random text does. Structural signal is real.

Eliminated

Tagalog or Philippine Languages

Boundary Concentration mismatch (0.20 vs 0.361). VMML unstable across texts. Cross-text Δ = 0.336 units.

Eliminated

Any Tested Austronesian Language

Indonesian (4.838), Malay (5.293), Cebuano NLLB (5.609) — all below the alphabetic ceiling.

Eliminated

Schinner's Stochastic Model

Random EVA generation fails to replicate the full 3-metric profile simultaneously.

Eliminated

Timm's Self-Citation Mechanism

EBNF/MI trade-off is irreconcilable with the observed structural pattern.

Eliminated

63+ Natural Language Profiles

No tested corpus from any language family simultaneously matches VMML, BC, and CBMI.
